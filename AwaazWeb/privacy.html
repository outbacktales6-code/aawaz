Privacy Policy

Effective Date: Today

Awaaz ("we," "us," or "our") was built with one fundamental goal: to protect your privacy and identity during critical situations. Because of this, our privacy policy is extremely simple: We do not track you.

1. What We Don't Collect

  • No Personal Information: We do not ask for your phone number, email address, or real name.
  • No Location Tracking: We do not collect or store your GPS location.
  • No Message Interception: All messages sent over the Awaaz mesh network are transmitted directly between devices (peer-to-peer). We do not have servers that route, read, or store your messages.

2. What We Do Collect

  • Anonymous Download Telemetry: To understand how widely the app is being used, we collect an anonymous ping when the app is installed. This contains no personally identifiable information.

3. Local Storage

Your messages are stored in an encrypted SQLite database locally on your own physical device. If you delete the app, your message history is permanently destroyed.

4. Changes to this Policy

If we ever update this policy, the core promise will remain the same: we will never track your identity.
